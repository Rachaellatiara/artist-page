Bio

Rachaelle C.E. is a contemporary artist specializing in oil paint portraiture. She attended the University of New Mexico for a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 2016 with a focus on painting. Her work includes both realism as well as abstract lines, with a high level of detail in every piece. C.E. uses soft value transitions and bold hues in order to offer the viewer an emotionally delicate, yet powerful viewing experience. C.E. was born in Albuquerque, New Mexico where she currently resides, and considers herself a citizen of the World.
